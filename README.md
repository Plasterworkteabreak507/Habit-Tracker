## 📝 What is Habit-Tracker?

Habit-Tracker helps you keep track of your daily habits. You enter data on whether you completed each habit. It shows your progress and helps you stay on track.

This app stores your habit data securely so you can trust your information is safe. It uses common tools to protect your data, even if you are new to computers.

## 🔒 Data Security

Your habit data is stored in a local SQLite3 database. The data is encrypted using a safe method called Fernet symmetric encryption. Passwords are protected using a salted hash, making it harder for anyone else to access your account.

## 🗄️ About the Database

Habit-Tracker uses SQLite3 to store your habits and progress. SQLite3 is a lightweight, local database that keeps your data on your computer.

This means your information stays with you unless you choose to share or export it.

## 🛡️ Security Features Explained

- **Fernet Encryption:** This locks your habit data so only the app can read it.

- **Password Hashing with Salt:** Your password is stored safely with extra random data to prevent hacks.

This makes your account and data secure even if someone accesses the files on your computer.
